I am excited that you are considering to join the MIND lab. Please review our lab values and this document to gain an understanding of how I train mentees. A brief highlight of qualities I am looking for are listed below.

The MIND lab is a great fit if you are (or are actively working on becoming)...

  1. Enthusiastic about learning and applying new statistical techniques to real-world problems.
  2. Open to understanding and improving your strengths and weaknesses.
  3. Ready to identify and overcome patterns that may hinder your productivity and growth.
  4. Self-motivated and driven to succeed.
  5. Able to work independently after an initial training period.
  6. A quick learner.
  7. Results-oriented and focused on outcomes.
  8. A critical thinker.
  9. Organized and detail-oriented.
  10. Committed to maintaining high research standards.
  11. An excellent communicator.

  1. You start projects at the last minute.
  2. You prefer thinking over taking action.
  3. You are not focused on self-improvement.
  4. You are not open to constructive feedback.

Opportunities Provided by the MIND Lab

  1. Rigorous statistical theory applied to real-world problems: All of our research is grounded in real applications, particularly related to neurodegenerative diseases. You will be trained in developing new statistical techniques and rigorous theory to address these challenges.
  2. Valuable coding skills: You will learn to implement methodologies in R, Matlab, C++, and Fortran, ensuring your code is efficient, well-documented, and organized. You will also contribute to open-source repositories like GitHub and CRAN, sharing your work with the broader scientific community.
  3. Persuasive communication skills: You will develop clear and compelling writing and presentation skills, allowing you to effectively communicate complex ideas to both statisticians and non-statisticians.
  4. Productivity and creativity enhancement: You will be trained to enhance both your productivity and creativity, producing high-quality work while enjoying the process. Our focus on personal and professional development will help you build lasting habits for success.
  5. Professional development and well-being: We meet monthly to discuss strategies that support both personal and professional growth. These sessions help address mental blocks, improve work strategies, and foster a supportive team environment. We also focus on building character skills such as resilience, creativity, and confidence to support long-term success.
  6. Collaborative, team-based research: You will have the opportunity to work on team-based research projects, developing and executing solutions that require diverse talents and perspectives. These projects encourage collaboration and help build strong working relationships with peers and mentors.
  7. Skills to become an indpendent researcher: You will receive leadership training to mentor others and advance your own professional development. You will also learn to become an independent researcher, mastering grant writing and securing funding. With my guidance, thirty-five mentees have so far won 70+ competitive awards, grants, and fellowships, and you will be supported in building successful grant proposals to boost your career.
  8. Supportive and inclusive environment: The MIND Lab prioritizes inclusivity and values diverse skills, perspectives, and backgrounds. You will be encouraged to bring your unique talents and experiences to help drive creativity and innovation.
