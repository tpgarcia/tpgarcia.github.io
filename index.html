Research Mission and Vision

The Methods for INcomplete Data (MIND) Lab advances statistical methods for handling censored, missing, or incomplete data to improve the estimation of neurodegenerative disease progression. We envision clinician-scientists using the methods we develop, built into open-access and reproducible software, to design clinical trials and help patients suffering from these devastating diseases.
